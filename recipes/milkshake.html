Banana Milkshake

A quick, nutritive and delicious recipe for all ages, you can adapt it to different milks and sweeteners, it's creamy and it doesn't require ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 3 ice cubes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
